  • Evidence Based Practice Portal

    Created for School of Nursing at Wichita State University

    This project was created in collaboration with faculty from the Wichita State University College of Health Professions. The Portal can be used as a stand-alone resource for students needing to enhance their skills, or as part of a course to teaching the principles of evidence based practice and information literacy.

    Formulating a Search Strategy Tutorial - this tutorial was created with Articulate Storyline and can also be used as a stand-alone learning object or embedded in a CMS.

Teaching Bootcamp for Library Staff

Over 40 library staff participated in a 2-day Teaching Bootcamp, which covered foundational instructional design skills and strategies for engaging students in active learning and assessment. Attendees participated in a number of workshops and activities designed to strengthen their teaching skills and help them develop new techniques for their training and classes in the fall.
